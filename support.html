QuietOne support and contact information

If you have questions, bug reports, or subscription issues, contact support by email.

Email: dplusa.ca@gmail.com
Recommended information to include:
  • device model;
  • iOS or watchOS version;
  • a short description of the issue;
  • steps to reproduce it, if relevant.

Typical response time: 1–2 business days.
